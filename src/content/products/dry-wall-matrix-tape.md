## Product Description

Dry Wall Matrix Tape is a high-strength fiberglass mesh reinforcement tape used for drywall joint treatment, crack repair, and surface reinforcement. Its self-adhesive mesh structure provides excellent bonding with joint compounds and plaster, helping create strong, smooth, and crack-resistant wall finishes.

Widely used by contractors, builders, and interior finishing professionals, it improves durability while reducing the risk of joint separation and surface cracking.
