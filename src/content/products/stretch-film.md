## Product Description

Stretch Film is a flexible polyethylene wrapping film used for securing cartons, pallets, and industrial goods. Its excellent stretchability and cling properties provide tight load containment, helping protect products from dust, moisture, dirt, and handling damage.

Widely used in warehouses, logistics, manufacturing units, and shipping operations, stretch film improves load stability while reducing packaging costs.

* LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene)
