## Product Description

Packing Seals (Clips) are manufactured from premium-grade metal and are used to securely lock PP and PET strapping during packaging operations. These seals provide strong holding power, ensuring that strapped cartons, pallets, and heavy loads remain secure during storage, handling, and transportation.

Suitable for manual strapping applications across warehouses, logistics centers, manufacturing units, and industrial packaging environments.
