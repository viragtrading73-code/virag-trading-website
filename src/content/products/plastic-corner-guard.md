## Product Description

Plastic Corners, also known as Corner Guards or Edge Protectors, are used to safeguard the edges and corners of packed goods during strapping, storage, and transportation. They help prevent crushing, denting, and strap damage while improving load stability.

Manufactured from durable plastic material, these corner protectors distribute strapping tension evenly across the package surface, making them ideal for palletized loads, cartons, furniture, building materials, and industrial products.
