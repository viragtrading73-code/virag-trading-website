## Product Description

Corrugated Corners are manufactured from premium corrugated paperboard to provide superior edge protection for packaged products. They help prevent corner crushing, edge damage, and deformation caused by strapping, stacking, and handling during transit.

Widely used in pallet packaging, carton protection, furniture packaging, and industrial shipments, corrugated corners improve load stability while maintaining a professional packaging appearance.
