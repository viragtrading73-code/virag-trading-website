## Product Description

Thermocol Sheets are manufactured from high-quality Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) and are widely used for protective packaging, thermal insulation, and cushioning applications. Their lightweight structure provides excellent shock absorption and impact resistance, helping protect fragile products during storage and transportation.

These sheets are also extensively used in construction, cold storage, insulation systems, and industrial packaging due to their thermal insulating properties and ease of handling.
