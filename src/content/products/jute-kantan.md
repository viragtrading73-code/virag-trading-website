## Product Description

Jute Kantan, also known as Hessian Cloth, is a durable woven fabric made from 100% natural jute fibres. It is commonly used for wrapping exposed roots of trees and shrubs during transplantation, protecting plants, and controlling soil erosion on slopes and embankments.

The fabric is naturally breathable, allowing air and moisture circulation while providing protection and support. Being completely biodegradable, Jute Kantan is an environmentally friendly solution for agricultural, landscaping, horticultural, packaging, and construction applications.
