## Product Description

Air Bubble Roll is a lightweight protective packaging material manufactured from premium polyethylene film with uniformly sealed air bubbles. It provides excellent shock absorption and cushioning, helping protect fragile and valuable products from damage during handling and transit.

Widely used in e-commerce, electronics, glassware, furniture, automotive, and industrial packaging applications, bubble rolls offer reliable protection while remaining lightweight and cost-effective.

* Polyethylene (PE) Film with Air Bubble Cushioning
