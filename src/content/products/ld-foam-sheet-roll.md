## Product Description

LD Foam Sheets & Rolls are manufactured from high-quality Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) foam, providing excellent cushioning and impact resistance. These foam products help protect delicate and valuable items from scratches, dents, vibrations, and transit damage.

Widely used in packaging, electronics, furniture, glass, automotive, and industrial applications, LD Foam offers reliable protection while remaining lightweight and easy to handle.
