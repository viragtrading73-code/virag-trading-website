## Product Description

Plastic Tarpaulin is a heavy-duty protective sheet designed to safeguard materials from rain, sunlight, dust, wind, and other environmental conditions. Manufactured from woven HDPE/PP fabric with protective lamination, it offers excellent durability, tear resistance, and long service life.

Widely used in agriculture, construction, transportation, warehousing, and industrial applications, tarpaulin sheets provide reliable protection for goods stored indoors and outdoors.
