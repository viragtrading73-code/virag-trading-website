## Product Overview

Virag Trading Co. is recognized as a premier supplier of premium **Plastic Tarpaulins (locally known as Tadpatri)** in Gujarat. Our tarpaulins are engineered from high-density polyethylene (HDPE) woven fabric, which is then laminated on both sides with low-density polyethylene (LDPE) to create an impenetrable, 100% waterproof barrier.

In the challenging climates of India, particularly during the intense monsoon seasons, protecting your raw materials, finished goods, and agricultural yields is paramount. Our heavy-duty tarpaulins act as a robust shield against torrential rain, scorching UV rays, dust, and wind. We cater specifically to B2B clients, including construction firms, logistics operators, agricultural cooperatives, and warehouse managers who require dependable, industrial-grade coverage solutions.

Whether you need lightweight 90 GSM sheets for temporary coverage or extreme heavy-duty 400 GSM cross-laminated tarpaulins for long-haul truck covers and structural roofing, our inventory is equipped to handle bulk requirements swiftly and efficiently. 

## Features

Our Plastic Tarpaulins are manufactured using state-of-the-art extrusion and weaving technology to deliver superior performance:
* **100% Waterproof & Weatherproof:** The double-sided LDPE lamination ensures zero water seepage, making it the perfect defense during the monsoon.
* **High Tensile & Tear Strength:** The tightly woven HDPE base fabric provides exceptional resistance to ripping, tearing, and puncturing, even under high wind pressure.
* **UV Stabilization:** Special UV inhibitors are added during manufacturing to prevent the plastic from turning brittle and cracking under prolonged exposure to the Indian sun.
* **Reinforced Edging:** The borders are folded and heat-sealed, often incorporating a strong nylon rope within the hem for added structural integrity.
* **Rust-Proof Eyelets:** Aluminum or heavy-duty plastic grommets are securely installed at regular intervals to allow for easy and secure tie-downs.
* **Rot, Mildew & Chemical Resistant:** The synthetic nature of the material prevents the growth of fungus and offers excellent resistance to most industrial chemicals and acids.
* **Flexibility in Extreme Temperatures:** Remains pliable and easy to handle in both freezing conditions and intense heat.

## Applications

The versatility of our HDPE tarpaulins makes them indispensable across a wide array of applications:
* **Construction & Infrastructure:** Covering cement stocks, steel rebars, scaffolding, and under-construction roofs to prevent weather damage and ensure work continuity.
* **Logistics & Transportation:** Heavy-duty covers for open trucks, railway wagons, and shipping cargo to protect goods in transit from rain and dust.
* **Agriculture & Farming:** Essential for covering harvested crops, fertilizers, and grain storage. Also used for drying spices and grains directly on the ground.
* **Warehousing & Storage:** Temporary roofing for open godowns, partition walls in factories, and protective covers for idle machinery.
* **Events & Tents:** Used as waterproof roofing and ground sheets for outdoor exhibitions, pandals, and emergency relief camps.
* **Aquaculture & Ponds:** High GSM variants are often utilized as economical pond liners for water harvesting and fish farming.
