## Product Description

Corrugated Sheets are manufactured from premium kraft paper and feature a fluted inner layer that provides excellent strength and cushioning. They are widely used for packaging, box manufacturing, protective layering, partitions, and surface protection in industrial and commercial applications.

The sheets help prevent damage from impact, vibration, and handling during transportation and storage, making them a reliable packaging solution.
