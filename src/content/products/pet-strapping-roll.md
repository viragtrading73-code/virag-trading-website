## Product Description

PET Strapping Roll is manufactured from premium-quality polyester material to provide superior strength and durability for heavy-duty packaging applications. It is an ideal alternative to steel strapping due to its high tensile strength, flexibility, and resistance to impact.

The strapping effectively absorbs shocks and maintains tension, making it suitable for securing pallets, construction materials, timber, bricks, metal products, and industrial shipments during storage and transportation.
