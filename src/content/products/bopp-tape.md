## Product Description

BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) Tape is a durable packaging tape designed for sealing cartons, boxes, and packages during storage and transportation.

Manufactured using premium BOPP film and strong adhesive coating, it provides excellent bonding, smooth unwinding, and reliable sealing performance.

Suitable for warehouses, logistics, e-commerce, manufacturing units, and industrial packaging applications.
